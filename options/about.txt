======== HACK THE ORBIT ============================================================================================================

    YEAR: 2086

    MISSION: Operation DarkSky
    In a distant future, a rogue AI has seized control of humanity's last communication lifeline, the NEXUS satellite network.
    As part of Operation DarkSky, your mission is to breach the system, expose its secrets, and dismantle the AI's control.

    Your weapon? Code.
    Your target? Satellites.

    GITHUB: https://github.com/ANG13T/hack-the-orbit

===================================================================================================================================

    COMMANDS:
    - ls: List files in the current directory
    - cd: Change directory
    - pwd: Print the current working directory
    - man: Display manual pages
    - help: List command information
    - exit: Exit the program
